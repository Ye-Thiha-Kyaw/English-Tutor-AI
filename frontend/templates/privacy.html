Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to English Tutor AI. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Conversation Data: Messages you send to our AI tutor for the purpose of providing English learning assistance.
  • Voice Data: Audio recordings when you use the voice chat feature, processed locally in your browser.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our service, pages visited, and features used.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and device type.
  • Cookies: Small files stored on your device to improve your experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our English tutoring service
  • To improve and personalize your learning experience
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our service
  • To display relevant advertisements
  • To communicate with you about updates or changes

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Groq API: For AI language processing. Your messages are sent to Groq's servers for processing.
  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your interests.
  • Web Speech API: For voice recognition, processed by your browser.

Data Retention

Conversation data is stored temporarily during your session and is not permanently saved on our servers. Session data is cleared when you close your browser or start a new conversation.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of personalized advertising
  • Disable cookies in your browser settings

Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for users of all ages for educational purposes. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
