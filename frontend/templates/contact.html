Frequently Asked Questions

Is English Tutor AI really free?

Yes! Our service is completely free to use. We're supported by advertisements, which allows us to offer English tutoring at no cost to you.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is needed. Just visit our website and start practicing immediately!

Is my conversation data saved?

Your conversation data is only stored temporarily during your session. When you refresh the page or close your browser, the conversation is cleared. We don't permanently store your messages.

Why isn't the voice feature working?

The voice feature requires a modern browser (Chrome, Edge, or Safari) and microphone permission. Make sure you've allowed microphone access when prompted.

Can I use this on my phone?

Yes! English Tutor AI works on mobile devices. For the best voice experience, we recommend using Chrome on Android or Safari on iOS.

How accurate is the grammar correction?

Our AI is highly accurate for most common grammar issues. However, like any AI, it may occasionally make mistakes. For important documents, we recommend additional human review.

Can I suggest new features?

Absolutely! We love hearing from our users. Send your suggestions to our email and we'll consider them for future updates.
