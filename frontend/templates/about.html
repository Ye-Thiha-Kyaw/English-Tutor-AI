About English Tutor AI

Your free AI-powered English language learning companion, available 24/7 to help you improve your speaking and writing skills.

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves access to quality English education. English Tutor AI was created to break down barriers to language learning by providing a free, accessible, and effective way to practice English anytime, anywhere.

What We Offer

Conversational Practice

Engage in natural conversations with our AI tutor on any topic. Practice speaking and writing in a judgment-free environment.

Grammar Correction

Get instant feedback on your grammar mistakes with clear explanations to help you understand and learn from errors.

Voice Interaction

Practice your pronunciation and speaking skills with our voice chat feature. Speak naturally and get responses read aloud.

Vocabulary Building

Learn new words and expressions in context. Our AI suggests better vocabulary choices to expand your language skills.

How It Works

  1. Start a Conversation

    Type a message or click the microphone to speak. Talk about any topic you like!

  2. Get Instant Feedback

    Our AI analyzes your English and provides corrections with helpful explanations.

  3. Practice & Improve

    Continue the conversation to practice. The more you use it, the more you learn!
